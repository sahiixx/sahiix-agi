## Data Quality Notes

### Issues Identified

1. **Star Count Discrepancies**: Some repos appear in multiple source reports with different star counts (e.g., `ollama/ollama` reported as 160k in trending vs 170k in landmark). We kept the highest count.
2. **Missing Star Counts**: 24 repositories have N/A or 0 stars, mostly academic papers (CVPR/ICLR/NeurIPS 2025) and very new projects.
3. **Category Ambiguity**: Some repos span multiple categories. We assigned the primary category based on their main purpose:
   - `n8n-io/n8n` categorized as Agents (not just workflow automation)
   - `langchain-ai/langchain` categorized as Agents (not just Frameworks in landmark report)
   - `sansan0/TrendRadar` categorized as Data in trending, Agents in recent → kept Agents as primary
